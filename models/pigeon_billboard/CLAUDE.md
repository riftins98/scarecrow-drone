# pigeon_billboard

Visual billboard target with a pigeon image, used as a YOLO detection target. Placed 5 m in front of the drone spawn in `drone_garage.sdf`.

## Files
- `model.sdf` — Flat plane geometry with the pigeon texture applied.
- `model.config` — Gazebo model manifest.

## Asset directories
- `materials/textures/` — `pigeon.jpg`, `pigeon.png`, `pigeon2.jpg`, `pigeon3.jpg` (pure image assets).
